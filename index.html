Got Questions?

FAQ

How do you access my vehicle to fuel it? +
You simply leave your vehicle parked with the fuel door accessible. Our certified technician will fuel your car and lock the cap securely when done. You don't need to be present.
What type of fuel do you deliver? +
At launch we deliver regular unleaded (87), premium (91), and diesel. If you have a specific fuel need, let us know on the contact form below and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Is this available for fleets and businesses? +
Yes — fleet and employer accounts are a core part of what we offer. Reach out via the contact form below to discuss volume pricing and access codes for your team.
How much does it cost? +
Fuel is priced at market rate, plus a small convenience fee for the delivery. Our goal is simple: the total cost should be close to what you'd pay at the pump — and a clear win when you factor in the time and hassle you save. Final pricing will be confirmed at launch, and reservation holders see it first.
How do I know when you're launching? +
Sign up above with your name, email, and zip code. We'll send you a notification as soon as we're live in your area — reservation holders get first access.
Is my vehicle information secure? +
Yes. Customer data is encrypted and never sold or shared. Fitzy is licensed and insured, and we operate to the safety standards required for fuel handling and delivery.
Who is behind Fitzy? +
Fitzy is locally owned and operated, started right here in the 805. We're launching in Lake Sherwood first because it's home — we'd rather get it right for our neighbors before we expand.
